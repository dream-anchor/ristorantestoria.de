STORIA – In the Heart of Munich

STORIA is more than a restaurant – it's a piece of Italy in the middle of Munich. Since 1995, the Speranza family has been welcoming guests in Maxvorstadt with authentic Italian hospitality, culinary passion and the unmistakable feeling of being welcome.

What began as a small family restaurant is today a permanent institution in Munich: a place where Italian lifestyle meets Bavarian warmth. STORIA's kitchen combines traditional recipes from Southern Italy with fresh, high-quality ingredients – from homemade pasta to Neapolitan pizza from the stone oven.

Our aspiration is to treat every guest as we would want to be treated ourselves: with attention, warmth and genuine interest in their well-being. Whether you're visiting us for the first time or have been one of our regulars for years – we want you to feel at home.

The central location in Maxvorstadt, just steps from Königsplatz and the Central Station, makes STORIA the ideal meeting point: for a relaxed lunch, an after-work aperitivo, a romantic dinner for two, or a big celebration with family and friends.

Our covered terrace offers a special ambiance even on cooler days – with a view of the lively activity on Karlstraße and the feeling of pausing for a moment while the city pulses around you.

STORIA is a place of encounter, enjoyment and hospitality. We invite you to become part of our story – and perhaps write your own story with us.

We don't just cook – we share our passion. Every dish that leaves our kitchen tells of the love for Italian cuisine, of family traditions and of the desire to bring people together through good food.

We look forward to seeing you.

Domenico Speranza
